======================================================================

Documentation:

- No source-code change is required in this documentation step.

Locked decisions:

1. COMMISSION
- Commission is calculated and accrued when a sale/return bill is posted.
- Actual payment happens later through a separate settlement/payment entry.
- Sale adds commission.
- Return reverses commission against the original seller.
- Net daily commission movement = current sale commission - return commission.
- Example: 5 sold at Rs 20 and 2 returned = Rs 60 net commission movement.

2. OWNER COMMISSION
- Owner commission is a real Commission Expense and separate Owner Commission Payable.
- It is not mixed with Owner Drawings.

3. SALE + RETURN
- The current bill may contain positive sale lines and negative return lines.
- Every negative line references the original posted bill/line.
- Original posted bills remain immutable.
- Backend creates linked return/audit/accounting reversal records.

4. OTHER SALE
- Add "Other Sale" as a fourth sale type.
- It is customer-account/credit sale.
- Customer mandatory.
- Payment may be zero/partial.
- Remaining amount becomes customer receivable.
- Later receipts link to customer/bill.

5. RIDER COD
- Owner/Admin/Accountant and Rider can record permitted delivery/return statuses.
- All status changes synchronize and are audited.
- On COD delivery:
  Rider COD Receivable / Cash with Rider Dr
  Customer Receivable Cr
- On settlement:
  Cash/Bank/Petty Cash Dr
  Rider COD Receivable / Cash with Rider Cr
- Partial return adjusts only returned quantity.

6. CONTRA & TRANSFERS
- One simple UI supports:
  - Internal Transfer
  - Owner Drawing
  - Capital Introduced
- Backend posts each type correctly.
- Owner Drawing is never posted as fake cash-to-cash Contra.
